Target audience

Elle&Gant appeals to people who seek out everyday, affordable, but also elegant jewelry. They are looking for casual pieces that they can add to any outfit that look expensive, but are reasonably priced.

They seek out quality that is high enough that the jewelry won’t turn green or break, but they don’t necessarily need real gold (for example).
