About

Hi! I'm Elle Woods, and I'm the founder of Elle&Gant. I was fed up with all my staple jewelry turning green. So I started Elle&Gant, a brand that prioritizes quality and affordability. I grew up in San Francisco and Walnut Creek, and I believe in shopping locally, so all of our jewelry is produced in the Bay Area. I love that I was able to bring my small business to my hometown, and will soon be able to expand to more cities in the area!
