BW Magazine

BW magazine showcases soft grunge fashion, originally inspired by the grunge fashion craze of the 1990s and recently revived.

Brands that speak the visual language of my brand are Kat Von D Makeup, Harley Davidson, Brandy Melville, Doc Marten, Inkbox, and Cigarettes after Sex.
