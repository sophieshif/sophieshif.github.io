Staple Items For Your Closet

Just because you're into fashion, doesn't mean you have to have a lot of clothes. Having a couple versatile clothing items that fit your taste is a great way to ease yourself into dressing better. Soft grunge, in particular, often reuses the same pieces to make different looks.

Our Favorite Soft Grunge Shoes

Even though they're at the bottom of your outfit, shoes are often the star of the show. In soft grunge fashion especially, shoes can make an outfit. Chunky, tall, or platform shoes can pull a look together.

Why You Should Try Shopping Second-hand

By now we've all heard about the thrifting craze. But shopping second-hand is not just a fleeting trend—it is a sustainable alternative to the fast-fashion industry.
